# AcreFi Whitepaper v0

## Abstract

AcreFi is a private agricultural credit protocol built on Midnight.
Its mission is to connect global liquidity with agricultural producers using verifiable but privacy-preserving financial infrastructure.

## Problem

Agricultural credit suffers from:
- high friction
- limited access
- poor data transparency
- lack of privacy

## Solution

AcreFi introduces a protocol that combines:
- private borrower evaluation
- agricultural receivables
- smart contract-based lending flows
- programmable compliance

## MVP

The initial AcreFi MVP focuses on:
- agricultural receivables
- milk income streams
- short-term productive credit

## Why Midnight

Midnight enables:
- selective disclosure
- privacy-preserving applications
- programmable financial logic

## Long-term vision

AcreFi aims to become a scalable credit infrastructure for agriculture, starting with receivables and later expanding to additional real-world agricultural assets.
